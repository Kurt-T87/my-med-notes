## 🧬 Gemini 临床深度解析

- **分型与人群:** 
  本研究聚焦于 **HER2-low（HER2低表达）** 乳腺癌人群，重点探讨该群体内部由激素受体（HR）状态定义的两个亚群：**HR+/HER2-low** 以及 **HR-/HER2-low**（临床行为更接近三阴性乳腺癌 TNBC）。研究旨在揭示这两类患者在分子机制上的异质性。

- **PICO 核心:** 
    - **P (Patient):** HER2低表达乳腺癌患者。
    - **I (Intervention):** 整合转录组学分析（miRNA与mRNA差异表达分析及生存分析）。
    - **C (Comparison):** HR阳性（HR+）与HR阴性（HR-）亚组之间的比较。
    - **O (Outcome):** 识别出165个差异miRNA和170个差异基因；发现关键调控轴（如miR-576-5p/TGFBI）；评估对总生存期（OS）和无进展间期（PFI）的影响。

- **临床价值:** 
    本研究在国际上**首次**通过miRNA-基因网络证实了HER2-low乳腺癌内部存在明显的转录组差异。这为“HER2-low并非单一生物学实体”提供了坚实的分子证据，支持临床上根据HR状态对HER2-low患者进行差异化管理。研究发现的特定miRNA网络可能成为未来精准分层的新生物标志物，或作为针对HR方向治疗的潜在新靶点。

- **关键数据:** 
    - **分子差异:** 筛选出 **165个** 显著失调的miRNA和 **170个** 显著失调的基因。
    - **核心靶点:** 识别出 **miR-9-5p、miR-532-5p 和 miR-576-5p** 与HR阴性状态密切相关。
    - **调控通路:** HR+ 亚组富集在激素相关通路；HR- 亚组富集在 **免疫、炎症及增殖** 通路。
    - **生存数据:** 生存分析显示 OS 和 PFI 虽有趋势，但**未达到统计学显著差异**（p > 0.05）。

- **专家评述:** 
    本研究虽然从分子层面解释了HER2-low患者中HR状态对预后和治疗反应影响的潜在机制，但仍存在一定局限：
    1. **生存动力不足:** 尽管识别出了显著的分子差异，但未能转化为统计学上的生存差异，这可能与样本量限制、随访时间或混杂因素有关。
    2. **回顾性偏倚:** 作为转录组整合分析，结果仍需在独立的前瞻性临床队列中进行验证。
    3. **后续关注点:** 重点应关注研究提出的 **miR-576-5p/TGFBI** 和 **miR-9-5p/POU2F2** 调控轴，通过功能实验验证其是否能作为逆转HR阴性HER2-low肿瘤侵袭性的治疗靶点。

## 📄 Abstract
<b>Background:</b> HER2-low breast cancer is a biologically heterogeneous subgroup in which hormone receptor (HR) expression critically shapes prognosis and treatment, but the underlying regulatory mechanisms remain unclear. MicroRNAs (miRNAs) are key post-transcriptional regulators of gene expression and may contribute to HR heterogeneity. This study aimed to identify deregulated miRNAs and associated gene networks distinguishing HER2-low/HR-positive from HER2-low/HR-negative tumors, elucidating the molecular mechanisms underlying this divergence. <b>Methods:</b> Differential expression analyses of miRNAs and genes were performed using Wilcoxon tests and DESeq2 (|log<sub>2</sub>FC| > 1; FDR-adjusted <i>p</i>-value < 0.05). Survival analyses were conducted using Cox proportional hazards models to evaluate the individual miRNAs and miRNA signature. Functional enrichment analyses, including GO, KEGG and Reactome pathways, were performed. Correlation analysis and the miRNA target prediction were integrated to identify regulatory interactions. <b>Results:</b> Comparisons between HER2-low/HR-positive and HER2-low/HR-negative tumors identified 165 significantly deregulated miRNAs and 170 strongly deregulated genes. Intersection analysis highlighted miR-9-5p, miR-532-5p and miR-576-5p as specifically associated with HR-negative status. Survival analyses showed non-significant trends for the overall survival and progression-free interval. Functional enrichment analysis revealed hormone-related pathways in HR-positive tumors and immune, inflammatory and proliferative pathways in HR-negative tumors. Integrative correlation and target prediction analyses identified two miRNA-mRNA regulatory axes, miR-576-5p/TGFBI and miR-9-5p/POU2F2. <b>Conclusions:</b> Our study demonstrated that HER2-low breast cancer exhibits distinct miRNA and gene expression profiles, which highlight different transcriptomic profiles according to HR status for the first time. Specific miRNA-gene networks may drive transcriptional heterogeneity, serving as potential biomarkers for stratification and as therapeutic targets. These findings provide insight into the molecular basis of HER2-low tumor diversity and support future development of HR-directed therapeutic strategies.
